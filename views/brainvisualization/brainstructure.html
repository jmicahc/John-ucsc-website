Physical Structure of the Brain

Description

Above is a visualization of the structure hierarchy of the brain in d3.js. Mouseover the region to highlight the associated brain region and pull up a tooltip containing wikipedia information about the reigion.

The size of each rectangle is related to the computed size of the brain region, at least in relative terms. A relative volume for each region was exproximated by adding up the calculated area of the associated svg image over every slice svg for which it was defined.

Sources:

  2. Neuroanatomical Data and Illustrations From The Allen Brain Atlas
